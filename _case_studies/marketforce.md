## Overview
As an Interim Engineer at MarketForce from May 2024 to September 2024, I played a pivotal role in maintaining and enhancing the company's B2B distribution platform. My primary focus was on modernizing the frontend architecture and delivering high-value, client-facing features.

## Key Responsibilities & Achievements

- **Feature Delivery:** Shipped 5+ critical client-facing features using React and TypeScript. These enhancements directly contributed to improving user engagement metrics across the platform by 25%.
- **Architecture Modernization:** Spearheaded a major frontend initiative to migrate legacy jQuery components into modern, maintainable React.js code. This refactoring effort reduced page load times by a significant 35% and cut the rate of incoming frontend bug reports in half.
- **Cross-Functional Collaboration:** Functioned seamlessly within a dynamic product team. Collaborated daily with 8 engineers, 2 designers, and product managers to consistently deliver sprint goals ahead of schedule.

## Impact
The modernization of the legacy frontend systems not only drastically improved the end-user experience but significantly enhanced the development velocity for the entire engineering team moving forward.
