What a weekend! Securing 1st Runners Up at the Helicode x GeniusLab Hackathon with Team University of Embu was an incredible experience. 

### The Challenge
We were tasked with building a solution that addresses critical needs in our community within a strict 48-hour window. Our team chose to focus on mental health, resulting in the birth of **MindFul Gen**.

### The Process
- **Hour 0-12**: Brainstorming and architecture planning.
- **Hour 13-36**: Intense development using Next.js, FastAPI, and Supabase.
- **Hour 37-48**: Polishing, testing, and preparing the pitch.

### The Win
The competition was fierce, and seeing the innovation from other teams was inspiring. Taking home the silver was a testament to our team's conviction and technical execution.

Onwards and upwards!
