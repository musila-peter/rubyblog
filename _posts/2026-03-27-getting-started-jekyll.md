Blogging is an essential part of a developer's growth. I've chosen Jekyll and the **Chirpy** theme to power my digital home.

### Why Jekyll?
It's static, fast, and generates my site from markdown files.

### Why Chirpy?
- Responsive and minimalist design.
- Built-in support for multiple post layouts.
- Excellent SEO-ready features.

---
Checkout the [docs](https://jekyllrb.com/) to start your own journey.
